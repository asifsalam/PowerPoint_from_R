Philippe Noiret (1930–2006)

Actor | Soundtrack | Miscellaneous Crew
Philippe Noiret was one of French cinema's most familiar faces who appeared in more than 140 films in a career that spanned more than half a century. Trivia:

He was the only French actor to play a major role in The Return of the Musketeers (1989). Ironically, his character Cardinal Mazarin was Italian in spite of the fact that he served as France's Chief Minister from 1642 to 1661. See more »
